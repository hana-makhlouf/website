Prototype Speed Radar Gun: Second Year Electronics Project

In order to measure the speed of a moving object, the frequency change was measured and fast fourier transforms were used to convert this to speed which was displayed on an LCD screen.

This was a group project with 3 coursemates. I was personally responsible for the logic gate circuit design and implementation. As part of that, I designed, simulated, built and tested a 7 segment decoder.
