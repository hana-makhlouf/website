Autonomous Robotic Car: First Year Engineering Project

The process involved designing and building several circuits, implementing computer vision using a Raspberry Pi and configuring several sensor circuits using Arduino.

This was a group project with 4 coursemates. Our group won second place in the poster competition as well as earning a place in the top 5 for accuracy of the robotic car. This was assessed through a race where all built cars had to overcome obstacles, measure the angle of an incline, stop at red traffic lights and shoot a ball into a goal.
